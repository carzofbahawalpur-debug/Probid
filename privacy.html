Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

At ProBid, we collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account. This includes your username, email address, encrypted password, and role selection (Admin, Vendor, or Freelancer). We also collect data related to your bidding history, including bid amounts and project descriptions, solely for the purpose of matching you with relevant opportunities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to authenticate your account and facilitate communication between Vendors, Freelancers, and Admins. Your bidding data is used to analyze market trends, calculate your "Market Fit Score" (if enabled), and display your profile statistics to potential employers. We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All passwords are hashed and salted using industry-standard encryption protocols. Access to your personal data is limited to employees who need to know that information in order to operate, develop, or improve our services.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

ProBid uses "cookies" to help personalize your experience. Cookies are small files placed on your computer's hard drive that track your preferences while you are on our site. These cookies allow us to recognize you each time you visit and help us compile aggregate data about site traffic so we can offer better features.

5. Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy. We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
